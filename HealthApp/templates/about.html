Meet the Developers

Our Story...

Singaporeans are on the lead of having an unhealthy eating lifestyle. Hence, to fight this lifestyle and to bring a positive change we have created an application. We are a group of developers who have created a calorie counting application with interactive AI features implemented and made it user-friendly for our users. The calorie counting feature identifies ever food object on the user's plate precisiely and lists out the calories to the user in the calorie counter table we have created. User's have the tendency of assuming the amount of calories they consume and because of that they loose track of their calories and end up gaining weight causing them to turn unhealthy and to be vulnerable to obesity.

Hence with our unique application that differes from other calorie counter applications, we hope users can positively benefit and get a the healthy-fit lifestyle they aspire to get.
