What does this App do?

This app allows users to record down their food intake by making them take picture of their meals and identifying each food item. After which, it will produce the amount of calories beside each image and users can see how much they have consumed. The reason for doing this is to allow users to see how much they actually are consuming as individuals have the tendency of presuming that they consumer lesser than the amount they actually take in.
